Pizza

Margarita Pizza

Is a dish of italian origin consisting of a usually round,
flat base of leavened wheat-based topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredient
such as (anchovies, meat, onions, olives, etc), which is then baked at a high temperature, tradionally
in wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.
A person who makes pizza is known as a pizzaiolo.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato
  • Basil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pizza Dough
  • Olive Oil
  • Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Make the base: Put the flour into a large bowl, stir in the yeast and salt. Make a well, pour in 200ml warm water and the olive oil a nd bring together with a wooden spoon until you have soft, fairly wet dough. Turn onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5 mins until smooth.
  2. Cover with a tea towel and set aside. You can leave the dough to rise if you like, but it's not essential f or a thin crust.
  3. Make the sauce: Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together, then season to taste. Leave to stand at room temperature while you get on with shaping the base.
  4. Roll out the dough: If you've let the dough rise, give it a quick knead, then split into two balls
  5. On a floured surface, roll out the dough into large rounds, about 25cm across, using a rolling pin. The dough needs to be very thin as it will rise in the oven. Lift the rounds onto two floured baking sheets.
  6. Top and bake: Hea t oven to 240C/fan 220C /gas 8
  7. Put another baking sheet or an upturned baking tray in the oven on the top shelf.
  8. Smooth sauce over bases with the back of a spoon. Scatter with cheese and tomatoes, drizzle with olive oil and season.
  9. Put one pizza, still on its baking sheet, on top of the preheated sheet or tray.
  10. Bake for 8-10 mins until crisp. Serve with a little more olive oil, and basil leaves if using. Repeat step for remaining pizza .
